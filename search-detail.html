DESCRIPTION

The Great Synagogue of Paris or Synagogue de la Victoire, inaugurated in 1874, has been listed as a historical monument since 1987. Seat of the Chief Rabbi of France and the Chief Rabbi of Paris, it is located in the 9th arrondissement.
